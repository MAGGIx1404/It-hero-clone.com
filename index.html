services

create it

We create websites basedon individual customer expectations.We design each page in a search engine-friendly way, in particular, focusing on its proper preparation for further positioning.

push it

We position websites and online stores by generating organic traffic,i.e. the most durable traffic that is obtained from search results. The effect of positioning is for your website to achieve the highest possible position in the result of the Google search engine for selected key phrases.

optimize it

We optimize websites (SEO optimization) on the basis of a detailed website audit containing individual guidelines aimed at improving the technical quality of the website. Correct optimization of the website will result in better digestibility of the website by the algorithms of the Google search engine.

advertise it

We run a Google Ads and Facebook Ads campaign, individually selecting a strategy to reach the recipients of your products and services. We maximize the sales results from your website based on the advertising budget planned by you.
